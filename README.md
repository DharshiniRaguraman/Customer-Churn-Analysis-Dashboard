# 📊 Customer-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard


## 📌 Project Overview
The Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze customer churn behavior and identify the key factors influencing customer retention. The dashboard transforms raw customer data into meaningful visual insights, enabling businesses to understand customer demographics, subscription patterns, service usage, and account information. By monitoring churn-related metrics, organizations can identify customers who are at risk of leaving and develop effective strategies to improve customer satisfaction and retention.

## 🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer churn patterns and trends. Identify high-risk customers based on account and service information. Understand customer demographics contributing to churn. Evaluate the impact of contract types, payment methods, and internet services on customer retention. Provide business stakeholders with an interactive dashboard for informed decision-making.

## 📊 Insights Derived
#### • Customers with Month-to-Month contracts show a higher churn rate compared to long-term contracts.
#### • Fiber Optic users represent a significant portion of churned customers.
#### • Customers raising more technical support tickets tend to have a higher probability of churning.
#### • Payment methods and billing preferences influence customer retention.
#### • Customer tenure plays a crucial role in predicting churn risk.
